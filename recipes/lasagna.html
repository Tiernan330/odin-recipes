Ingrediants

  • 1 (16 ounce) package of lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound of ground beef
  • salt and pepper
  • 1 (16 ounce) jar of spaghetti sauce
  • 1 clove of garlic, minced
  • 1/2 pounds of shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 pounds of shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 pint of ricotta cheese

Directions

  1. Bring a large pot of lighly salted water to boil. Add pasta and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until al denta; drain
  2. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). In a large skillet over medium-high heat, brown beef and season with salt and pepper; drain. stir in spaghe sauce and garlic and simmer 5 minutes.
  3. In a medium bowl, combine mozzarella, Cheddar and ricotta; stir well. In 9x13 inch pan, alternate layers of noodles, meat mixture and cheese mixture until pan is filled.
  4. Bake in preheated oven for 30 minutes, or until cheese is melted and bubbly.
